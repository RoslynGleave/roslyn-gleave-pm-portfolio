ERP Implementation — Odoo

Led end-to-end ERP implementation across sales, inventory, purchasing and fulfilment — replacing fragmented spreadsheet-based operations with a unified platform.

5+ Business Functions Unified
100% Inventory Visibility
1 Unified Platform

Overview

A multi-channel ecommerce business operating across direct-to-consumer, wholesale and marketplace channels had outgrown its spreadsheet-based operations. Inventory was fragmented across disconnected systems, financial reporting was manual, and order management lacked the structure needed to support continued growth. This project delivered a full Odoo ERP implementation to unify operations under a single platform.

The Challenge

  • Inventory managed across spreadsheets, ecommerce back-ends and warehouse records with no single source of truth
  • Order processing workflows were inconsistent across channels — direct, wholesale and marketplace each had different manual processes
  • Financial reporting required manual data aggregation from multiple sources
  • Purchasing decisions were made without reliable stock-level data, leading to overstocking and stockouts
  • No integrated system to support business scaling across new channels and product lines

My Role

Founder and systems delivery lead. Owned the full implementation lifecycle from vendor evaluation and selection through to data migration, configuration, testing, go-live and post-launch optimisation.

  • Evaluated ERP platforms and selected Odoo based on cost, flexibility and integration capabilities
  • Defined requirements across sales, inventory, purchasing, manufacturing and accounting modules
  • Led data migration planning — mapping legacy data structures to Odoo schema
  • Configured workflows, user roles and reporting dashboards
  • Coordinated testing across all business functions and managed phased rollout

Solution Delivered

Implemented Odoo ERP across core business functions, creating a unified operational platform. Key modules and integrations included:

  • Sales and CRM — centralised customer and order management
  • Inventory and warehouse — real-time stock tracking across all channels and locations
  • Purchasing — automated reorder rules and supplier management
  • Accounting — integrated financial reporting and bank reconciliation
  • Ecommerce integration — connected Shopify storefront to ERP for order and inventory sync
  • Data migration — historical customer, product and financial data migrated from legacy systems

Commercial / Operational Outcome

  • Unified 5+ business functions under a single platform — eliminating data silos
  • Achieved real-time inventory visibility across all sales channels for the first time
  • Reduced stockout and overstock incidents through automated purchasing rules
  • Streamlined financial reporting — month-end close time reduced significantly
  • Created a scalable operational foundation that supported business growth and marketplace expansion
  • Improved decision-making through accurate, real-time operational data
