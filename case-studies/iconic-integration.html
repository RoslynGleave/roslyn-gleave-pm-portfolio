Integration

The Iconic — Marketplace Integration Automation

Built a custom integration connecting ecommerce, ERP and The Iconic marketplace — automating order flow, inventory sync and fulfilment to support 3x revenue growth without increasing headcount.

~3x Sales Growth Enabled
0 Additional Headcount
100% Order Processing Automated

Overview

Deshabille was scaling rapidly through The Iconic marketplace alongside its existing direct-to-consumer ecommerce channels. As order volumes grew, the manual processes connecting marketplace orders to ERP, inventory and fulfilment systems became a bottleneck that would limit growth and increase operational risk.

This project delivered one of the first custom integrations connecting a Shopify-based ecommerce platform, Odoo ERP and The Iconic marketplace — creating an automated pipeline for orders, inventory and fulfilment.

The Challenge

Before the integration, the business faced critical operational constraints:

  • Orders from The Iconic were processed manually — entered by hand into the ERP system
  • Inventory quantities were managed via spreadsheets and updated manually across channels
  • Overselling was a constant risk, with no real-time sync between marketplace and warehouse stock levels
  • Fulfilment status updates were communicated manually back to The Iconic
  • Financial reconciliation was slow due to disconnected data between marketplace, ERP and accounting

The process could not scale beyond current volumes without adding staff — and even then, manual processing introduced unacceptable error rates for a premium brand.

My Role

Founder and systems delivery lead. Responsible for the full integration lifecycle — from identifying the opportunity and defining requirements through to vendor sourcing, solution design, testing and go-live support.

  • Mapped end-to-end order and fulfilment workflows across marketplace, ERP and 3PL systems
  • Defined integration requirements and API specifications
  • Sourced and managed external developer for middleware build
  • Coordinated with The Iconic technical team and 3PL warehouse partner
  • Led UAT, defect resolution and phased go-live

Solution Delivered

Designed and delivered a middleware integration layer connecting The Iconic marketplace API, Odoo ERP and the third-party logistics warehouse management system. Key components:

  • Automated order ingestion from The Iconic into Odoo ERP with field mapping, validation and error handling
  • Real-time inventory synchronisation between Odoo and The Iconic — preventing overselling across channels
  • Automated fulfilment and tracking updates from the 3PL warehouse back to The Iconic marketplace
  • Returns processing automation with stock-level adjustments
  • Error queuing, retry logic and alerting for failed transactions

Commercial / Operational Outcome

The integration fundamentally changed the operational capacity of the business:

  • Enabled approximately 3x marketplace revenue growth without hiring additional operations staff
  • Eliminated manual order entry — processing time dropped from hours to minutes
  • Inventory accuracy improved significantly, reducing oversell incidents to near zero
  • 3PL fulfilment feedback loop eliminated manual tracking updates entirely
  • Financial reconciliation became faster and more reliable through consistent, automated data flows
  • Protected marketplace revenue by maintaining The Iconic's performance and fulfilment SLAs
