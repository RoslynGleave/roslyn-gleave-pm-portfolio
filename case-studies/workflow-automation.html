Automation

Workflow Automation — Ecommerce & CRM

Designed and implemented workflow automation across ecommerce, email marketing and CRM platforms — reducing manual effort and enabling scalable digital operations.

60% Manual Workflows Eliminated
3x Lead Capture Improvement
15+ Automations Deployed

Overview

A growing retail consulting business needed to professionalise its digital operations — from ecommerce fulfilment to email marketing and CRM management. Manual processes were limiting the team's capacity to scale, with customer data fragmented across platforms and campaign workflows requiring constant manual intervention.

This project delivered a connected automation framework across Shopify, Zapier, Mailchimp and supporting tools — creating reliable, repeatable workflows that freed the team to focus on strategy and growth.

The Challenge

The business was running its digital operations with a collection of disconnected tools and manual processes:

  • Customer data was scattered across Shopify, spreadsheets and email platforms with no single source of truth
  • Email campaigns and marketing automations were triggered manually, creating delays and inconsistencies
  • Lead capture forms fed into spreadsheets rather than automated CRM workflows
  • Order notifications, customer follow-ups and review requests were handled by hand
  • The team was spending more time on admin than on revenue-generating activities

My Role

Digital delivery consultant responsible for automation strategy, platform configuration and implementation. Led the end-to-end process from current-state analysis through to deployment and handover.

  • Mapped current-state workflows and identified automation opportunities across marketing, sales and operations
  • Defined automation logic and trigger conditions for each workflow
  • Configured Zapier integrations connecting Shopify, Mailchimp, Google Sheets and internal tools
  • Designed email automation sequences for customer lifecycle touchpoints
  • Conducted UAT and provided training documentation for handover

Solution Delivered

Deployed a connected automation framework using Zapier as the middleware layer, linking ecommerce events to downstream marketing and operational workflows. Key automations included customer data sync between platforms, triggered email sequences for post-purchase and re-engagement, lead capture routing from web forms to CRM, and operational notifications for order status changes.

Commercial / Operational Outcome

The automation framework reduced manual workflow overhead by approximately 60%, improved lead capture reliability and enabled the team to scale marketing activity without adding headcount. Campaign consistency improved through automated triggers rather than manual scheduling.
