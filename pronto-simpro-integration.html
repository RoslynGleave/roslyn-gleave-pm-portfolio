ERP & Platform Transformation — Pronto & Simpro

Enterprise finance and operations integration project — eliminating manual invoice and purchase order processing between Simpro field services and Pronto ERP.

80% Reduction in Manual Entry
2hrs Daily Admin Saved
99% Data Accuracy Achieved

Overview

Climatech, a multi-division mechanical services and construction business, operated Simpro for field operations and job management alongside Pronto ERP for finance, procurement and reporting. The two systems were disconnected — creating manual handoffs for purchase orders, invoices and financial reconciliation that consumed significant admin time and introduced data quality risks.

This project delivered a custom API integration to automate data flows between Simpro and Pronto, focusing on invoice and purchase order synchronisation.

The Challenge

The disconnect between Simpro and Pronto created several operational bottlenecks:

  • Invoice data from Simpro was manually re-entered into Pronto ERP — a time-consuming and error-prone process
  • Purchase orders raised in Pronto had no automated link back to Simpro job costing
  • Finance team spent hours daily reconciling data between the two systems
  • Discrepancies between systems created reporting inaccuracies and delayed month-end close
  • High transaction volumes amplified every manual process bottleneck

My Role

Technical Business Analyst and project lead, acting as the bridge between business stakeholders (CFO, finance team, operations managers) and the development resource. Responsible for:

  • Business process mapping — documented current-state invoice and PO workflows end-to-end
  • Requirements gathering through stakeholder workshops with finance, operations and IT
  • Integration scoping — defined API endpoints, data mappings, validation rules and error handling
  • Produced Functional Requirements Specification (FRS) for developer handoff
  • Designed and coordinated SIT and UAT test frameworks
  • Managed release planning, go-live oversight and post-deployment stabilisation

Solution Delivered

Designed and delivered a REST API integration between Simpro and Pronto ERP with the following components:

  • Automated invoice synchronisation — Simpro-generated invoices pushed to Pronto with full GL code mapping
  • Purchase order data flow — POs created in Pronto automatically linked to Simpro job records
  • Validation rules to catch data discrepancies before they entered the ERP
  • Error queue with retry logic and alerting for failed transactions
  • Reconciliation reporting to give finance visibility into sync status and exceptions

Commercial / Operational Outcome

  • Eliminated approximately 80% of manual invoice and PO data entry
  • Freed approximately 2 hours of daily finance admin capacity
  • Achieved 99%+ data accuracy between Simpro and Pronto through automated validation
  • Reduced month-end reconciliation time significantly
  • Improved financial reporting accuracy and timeliness
  • Created a scalable integration pattern that could be extended to other Pronto modules
